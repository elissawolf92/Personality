Low-Level Parameter Tuning for Effort and Shape

 

This study aims to find a mapping between low-level motion parameters and Laban parameters. The study is composed of two parts: Parameter tuning for Shape and parameter tuning for Drives. You can switch between study modes by selecting/deselecting the "Switch study mode" toggle.

 

 Instructions:

1.     Please select a unique user name. 

2.     Please select your gender and write your age in the text box.

3.     Press the "Start" button. 

 

Study 1

1.     This study asks you to tune motion parameters so that the posture of the stationary model reflects the desired Shape.

2.      There are 13 sliders for rotating different body parts around various axes. Please adjust these sliders to convey the Shape stated on the screen. You need to perform these adjustments for 6 different Shape parameters: enclosing, spreading, sinking, rising, retreating and advancing.

3.      You can reset each parameter to its default value by pressing the “Reset” button near that parameter.

4.      Once you finish adjusting a trait, press "Submit" button at the bottom of the screen.

5.      You can move on to the next question by pressing the "Next question" button. You can go back by pressing the "Previous question" button.

6.      The study is over when you finish answering all the 6 questions for each Shape. (Whether you have submitted an answer or not to a question will appear on the screen.)

 

 

Study 2

1.      This study asks you to tune motion parameters so that animated motion reflects the desired Drive.

2.      There are 18 sliders for adjusting low-level motion parameters. Please adjust these sliders to express the Drive stated on the screen. You need to perform these adjustments for 32 different Drives.

3.      You can reset each parameter to its default value by pressing the “Reset” button near that parameter.

4.      Once you finish adjusting a trait, press "Submit" button at the bottom of the screen.

5.      You can move on to the next question by pressing the "Next question" button. You can go back by pressing the "Previous question" button.

6.      The study is over when you finish answering all the 32 questions for each Drive. (Whether you have submitted an answer or not to a question will appear on the screen.)

 

NOTE: Some parameters are meaningful only when combined with other parameters. For example, you can observe changes in wrist extension  when you modify wrist bend. Other related parameters are, head rotation magnitude - head frequency, wrist twist -wrist frequency, and elbow twist - elbow frequency.

 

Controls:

 

Mouse:

·        Left mouse + left alt: Orbit

·        Right mouse + left alt OR wheel: Zoom in/out

·        Middle mouse : Pan

Keyboard:

·       ‘A’: Increases the value of the selected parameter. (Parameter selection is done via the checkbox near each slider.)

·       ‘S’: Decreases the value of the selected parameter. (Parameter selection is done via the checkbox near each slider.)

·       Left and right keys:

o   Change Shape (Study 1)

o   Change effort combination (Drive) (Study 2)
